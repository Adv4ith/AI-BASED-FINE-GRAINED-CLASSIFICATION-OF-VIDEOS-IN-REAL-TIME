Full-Stack Surveillance Intelligence

End-to-end pipeline from raw video to natural language incident reports.

🎯
Person Detection
YOLOv8 real-time person detection with ByteTrack multi-person tracking across frames.
🎬
Action Tube Generation
Per-track 32-frame temporal buffers with ghost padding, stride gating, and smooth eviction.
🧠
Hierarchical ViViT
Binary gate → fine-grained 5-class routing. Three separate ViViT models for Normal, Abnormal, and Binary classification.
🔥
GradCAM++ Explainability
Spatial attention heatmaps from Stage-2 fine-grained classifier. Full-frame event localisation video output.
📈
Temporal Analysis
Frame-wise attention scoring, peak detection, activity onset/offset, and trend direction analysis.
🤖
LLM Surveillance Reports
Llama 3.1-8B generates natural language analysis for every track with risk assessment and recommended actions.
